Inertia of motion

Movement, feelings and dynamic visuals combined together into a concept allowing On's audience to express their passion for active lifestyle. Rhythmic photographs and colourful graphic, celebrate moments experienced throughout workout and progress. This concept aims to encapsulate the fluidity of motion, progress of the energy, and the vibrancy of self-expression - strengthening On's community bonds.
