Hi! My name's Kiniek and I'm an interdisciplinary designer specialising in branding and advertising. I am currently studying BA in Graphic and Digital Design at the University of Greenwich. In my practice, I like to experiment with a variety of disciplines, media, and techniques.


I see design as a practice of communication and problem-solving through engaging visuals — that’s what I do, with a special focus on the emotional aspect of each project.


The projects I’m most passionate about often explore the concept of shared experiences, whether related to social injustice, political issues, vibrant communities or unique cultural traditions.


Outside of design, I’m a part-time cook and a devoted rock collector.


I’m also a proud owner of three very large cats (1:200 scale).

My pronouns are he/him.


I am committed to fostering an inclusive and respectful practice, where discrimination, harassment, or any form of harm to others is not tolerated.
