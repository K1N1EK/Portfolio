Gays aren't evil

The idea behind this project is based on the practice of bootlegging and reformatted design. It’s my response to a harmful and obnoxious questionnaire found at the Bishopsgate institute. I found the questionnaire to be transphobic and stereotypical. In collaboration with Gray Good, who based her work off a newspaper article titled “Gays are evil” we created a set of conjoined screen printed garments. Then we each designed a reformatted piece of Risograph print - in my case an informative zine calling transgender people to fight for their rights and respect.
